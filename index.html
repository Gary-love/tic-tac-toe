A game of Tic-Tac-Toe

Welcome to tic-tac-toe:

Game Setup: There are two players, player 1 will be X and Player 2 will be O

- The goal is to be the first player to get three of your symbols in a row. This can be done horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

- The game board is divided into nine squares, and players take turns placing their symbols on the board.

Rules:

1. Starting the Game: player 1 starts first.

2. Taking Turns: Players alternate turns, placing their symbol (X or O) in an empty square on the grid.

3. Winning: A player wins if they place three of their symbols in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally).

4. Draw: If all nine squares are filled and no player has three in a row, the game is a draw.

5. Invalid Moves: Players cannot place their symbol in a square that is already occupied.
